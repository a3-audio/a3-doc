# A³ Mix usermanual
4-Channel DJ Mixer (Controller) with 3d toggle button per channel and fx engine. 

## Connectivity
- Rear: 2 XLR-Inputs for Headphone-signal from A³ Core
- Rear: 1 Cat for PoE and Communication with A³ Core (LAN)
- Front: Headphone jack 6.3mm

## Channelsection
- Poti: Gain
- Poti: Eq hi
- Poti: Eq mid
- Poti: Eq low
- Button: Cue (pfl)
- Poti: Channel volume
- Button: 3d toggle
- Button: fx toggle

## Mastersection
- Poti: Master Volume
- Button: Cue (afl)
- Poti: Booth
- Poti: Headphone mix
- Poti: Headphone volume

## Fx-section
- Poti: Filter frequency
- Poti: Filter resonance
- Button: Fx mode toggle (hi pass / lo pass)
- Button: 3d toggle on/off
