## Housing v0.2
The housing was build with Blender (*.obj) and is ready to print on a 3d-printer (*.stl).
- Blender project:
```
Ambijockey/hardware/moc/housing
├── moc_housing_bottom.obj
├── moc_housing_bottom.stl
├── moc_housing_top.obj
└── moc_main_pcb.stl
```
